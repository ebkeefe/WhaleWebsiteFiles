Whale Whale Whale what do we have here?

Whales range in size from the 2.6 metres (8.5 ft) and 135 kilograms (298 lb). The blue whale is the largest creature to have ever lived. Baleen whales have no teeth; instead they have plates of baleen, a fringe-like structure used to expel water while retaining the krill and plankton which they feed on. They use their throat pleats to expand the mouth to take in huge gulps of water. Balaenids have heads that can make up 40% of their body mass to take in water. Toothed whales, on the other hand, have conical teeth designed for catching fish or squid.
